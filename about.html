The Academy

Private Training, Proven Results

Flemington Footy Academy was founded with one mission: to provide athletes of all ages and skill levels with the high-quality, personalised training they need to reach their peak performance.

Whether you're a youth athlete looking to develop your foundational movement skills, a high school player trying to earn a starting spot, or an adult athlete maintaining your competitive edge — this is the place for you.

We keep sessions small and focused on purpose. That means more reps, more feedback, and faster progress for every athlete who walks through our doors.

Danny White
Head Trainer & Founder

Coach Danny White is a B Licensed Soccer Coach, Fitness Specialist, F.A. Level One Talent Scout and Exercise Science graduate currently coaching elite youth athletes at Rutgers University, Player Development Academy, Cheshire Soccer Academy and FC Copa. Using a background in fitness and player driven approach, Coach Danny aims to provide real evidence of development from each player by the end of training and prepare them for the next evolution of their game.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These principles guide every session, every program, and every interaction at Flemington Footy Academy.

01

Athlete First

Every decision is made with the athlete's long-term development and well-being at the forefront. No shortcuts, no ego.

02

Personalisation

One size does not fit all in sports training. Every program is built around the individual athlete's needs and goals.

03

Accountability

We hold athletes to high standards and support them in achieving more than they thought possible.

04

Continuous Improvement

Progress is tracked, measured, and celebrated. We believe in constant growth for both athletes and coaches.
