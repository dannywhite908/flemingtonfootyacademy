Available Class Lineup

Explore age-based classes designed to build strong soccer habits, confidence, and skill progression.

🪺

Next-2-Nest

Ages 3-4 years old

Focused on fun, dribbling, coordination, balance, socialization, and confidence.

✈️

Flight Club

Ages 5-8 years old

Focused on fun, ball skills, passing, receiving, athleticism, and weak foot development.

🦅

Eagle Futures

Ages 9-11

Focused on fun, positioning, scanning, combination play, and footwork.

🛡️

Talon Academy

Ages 12-13

Focused on possession, movement, set piece, communication, and speed/agility.

⚡

Speed & Agility

Ages 8+

Focused on speed, agility, balance, coordination, and rhythm.
