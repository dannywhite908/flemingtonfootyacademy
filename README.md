# Flemington Footy Academy

Elite private athletic training focused on speed, agility, and sport-specific performance.

## How to Request a Training Session

**No app. No instant bookings. Just you, your goals, and us.**

We believe the best training starts with a real conversation. Here's how it works:

### 1. Submit a Training Request
Visit the **"Request Private Training"** button on any page (or go directly to [contact.html](contact.html)). Fill out a short form with:
- Your athlete's name and age
- Sport and experience level
- Membership preference (Community Member, Blue & White Member, or Eagle Membership)
- Preferred training days
- Your goals and what you want to achieve

### 2. We Reach Out (within 24 hours)
We'll call or email you to:
- Confirm we're a good fit for your goals
- Find a specific time that works for your schedule
- Answer any questions about pricing, cancellations, or programs
- Take payment info (Cash, Venmo, or Zelle on the day of session)

### 3. You're Set
Your session is confirmed, locked in, and personalized to your athlete.
