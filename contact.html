Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

🏃

What age groups do you train?

We work with athletes of all ages — from youth players developing foundational skills to adult athletes maintaining peak performance.

🏅

What sports do you specialise in?

We work across all sports. Training is tailored to the movement demands and competitive requirements of your specific sport.

👕

What should I bring?

Wear athletic clothing and sport-appropriate footwear. Bring water and a positive attitude — we'll take care of the rest.

💳

When do I pay?

Payment is due at the time of your session. We accept Cash, Venmo, and Zelle for your convenience.

❌

What's your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. This ensures we can offer the slot to other athletes.

📦

Do you offer packages?

We're happy to discuss multi-session packages. Get in touch through the contact form or reach out directly to find out more.
